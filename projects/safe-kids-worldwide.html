Safe Kids Worldwide

Public Service Campaign

Safe Kids Worldwide is an organization that dedicates its efforts to preventing injuries in children internationally. The organization was in need of a new campaign to promote car seat safety and learning the proper way to use car seats safely. The audience Safe Kids Worldwide is targetting consists of first-time parents with children who are still small enough to need a car seat. They have already been using a car seat with their child, but might not be completely informed of all the safety precautions they need to consider. They put the needs of the children first and are concerned for their wellbeing above everything else.
