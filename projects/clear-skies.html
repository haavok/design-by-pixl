Clear Skies

Brand Identity & Responsive Brand Style Guide

Clear Skies is a small smoke shop business located in Lincoln, Nebraska. The shop specializes in the sale of electronic cigarettes, or “vapes,” and glass tobacco pipes. They were in need of an identity that represented the top-notch quality of their products. The audience that Clear Skies was targeting includes smokers between the ages of 20–40. These people like to savor their smoke and are willing to pay for quality products. These people live in modern, downtown apartments and pride themselves on appearance, of both themselves and their home. They expect the finest products and service for their money, and do not accept anything less.
