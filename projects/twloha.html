To Write Love On Her Arms

Annual Report

TWLOHA is a non-profit organization dedicated to bringing hope to those who suffer from depression, suicidal thoughts, and addiction. Over the years, this organization has helped shine a light on the cause and has been a leader in destroying the stigma that is associated with mental illnesses. TWLOHA was in need of a way to visually convey the achievements the organization has accomplished over the past year to their donors and those people who are invested in the cause.
