Oh, Snap! Photography

Promotional Booklet

Oh, Snap! Photography is a studio based in Lincoln, Nebraska and specializes in vintage style digital images. They were in need of a small, promotional booklet to entice new clients at trade shows. The target audience that Oh, Snap wished to reach includes young couples and families, aged 25–35, that are looking for a unique and fun photo session experience to capture their memories. They have an eye for a vintage look, but appreciate modern conveniences and quality.
