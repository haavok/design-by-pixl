Hyde Memorial Observatory

Ad Campaign

Hyde is a completely volunteer run organization that is dedicated to educating the general public of Lincoln, Nebraska in the area of astronomy. They were in need of a campaign to promote the observatory and to spread the word about their organization. Hyde’s target audience consists of men and women aged 25–35 who may not know much about astronomy, but are eager to learn. They likely have school-aged children and are interested in giving them a learning experience outside of a classroom.
