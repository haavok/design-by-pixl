Oh, Snap! Photography

Responsive Website Design

Oh, Snap! Photography is a studio based in Lincoln, Nebraska and specializes in vintage style digital images. They were in need of a portfolio site to showcase their work for their clients. The target audience that Oh, Snap wished to reach includes young couples and families, aged 25–35, that are looking for a unique and fun photo session experience to capture their memories. They have an eye for a vintage look, but appreciate modern conveniences and quality.

My Process

I use an iterative process that includes wireframes, style tiles, and pixel-by-pixel comps. This allows me to address user experience, layout, or other visual issues well before the website goes into development. Each step helps to streamline the process for more efficient work.
