Honest Abe’s Burgers & Freedom

Mobile App

Honest Abe’s is a small burger and fries restaurant located in Lincoln, Nebraska. Because of the restaurant’s success, they are constantly busy and with limited seating, it is sometimes a long wait for customers’ food. They needed a way to get customers their food more quickly, and the solution came in the form of a mobile app that allows the customer to order ahead and pick up once the food is ready. The audience that Honest Abe’s is targeting are their current customers, as well as those who have previously been turned off by the business of the restaurant. These people are in a hurry, they want a streamlined and simple process.
