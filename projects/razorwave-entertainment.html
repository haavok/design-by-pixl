Razorwave Entertainment

Brand Identity & Identity System

Razorwave Entertainment is a talent management and event booking business based in Omaha, Nebraska. They deal mostly with rock and metal bands and have a passion for social activism, often holding fundraisers for various non-profit organizations and causes within their community. They were in need of a new logo and identity system that would reflect their strong values but still have that “rock” feeling that their most loyal clients look for. The audience that Razorwave targets are mostly rock and metal fans, aged 20–35 who care for their community and are passionate about raising awareness for causes that matter to them.
